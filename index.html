Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing and buying furniture for your home

How do I measure my space to choose the right furniture?

Measure the dimensions of the room, including length, width, and height. Also, measure doorways, hallways, and staircases to ensure the furniture can be easily moved into the room. Use these measurements to select furniture that fits comfortably within the space without overcrowding.

What should I consider when choosing furniture for a small room?

Opt for multifunctional furniture, such as a sofa bed or storage ottomans. Choose pieces with a smaller scale and lighter appearance to avoid overwhelming the space. Light colors and reflective surfaces can help make the room feel larger.

How can I ensure my furniture matches my home’s style?

Identify your home’s design style (e.g., modern, traditional, eclectic) and choose furniture that complements this style. Look for consistent elements in color, material, and form. Mixing styles can work if there’s a unifying theme, such as color or texture.

What materials are best for durability?

Hardwood, metal, and high-quality fabrics are generally durable. For upholstery, look for materials like leather, microfiber, or performance fabrics that can withstand wear and tear. Check for solid construction and high-quality finishes for long-lasting furniture.
